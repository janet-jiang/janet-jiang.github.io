About Me

I am a senior at Duke University, advised by Prof. Kristin Stephens-Martinez.
I am interested in Computing Education Research (CER) and am hoping to pursue a Ph.D. in Computer Science. My research interests lie in providing equitable access to CS education and re-evaluating the effectiveness of commonly used pedagogy at facilitating learning in students from underrepresented groups. I am currently finalizing my honors thesis, which examines the relationship between student modality, discussion, and learning from peer instruction across modalities of hybrid courses. My other active research projects focus on the fairness of online CS assessments and the impact of ecological belonging interventions on introductory programmers.
