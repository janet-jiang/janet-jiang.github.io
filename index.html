About Me

I am a rising senior at Duke University, advised by Professor Kristin Stephens-Martinez.

My research is in computer science education, and my senior thesis focuses on the effectiveness of peer instruction in hybrid courses. In addition, I am interested in making computing and computing science curricula more accessible, and I hope to identify (and resolve!) barriers that marginalized students face entering and persisting in computing related fields.
